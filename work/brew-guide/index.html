The Concept & Branding

App Overview

This app is a communication tool between Ottawa breweries and their customers to notify them about new beers and locations to buy them, as well as inform consumers on all the Ottawa breweries and the beers they produce.

This app is meant to be Ottawa specific, targeting the locals in this exponentially growing craft brewery scene in Ottawa, as well as craft-hungry tourists.

Right now, it’s hard to find exactly what is available from specific brewery and where to buy it. This app looks to satisfy this need, making access to information easier, thereby increasing sales and consumption for the breweries and their retailers as a result.

Branding Overview

The branding of the app captures key characteristics of the Ottawa craft beer scene. It captures the retro connection to quality, homemade products, and community. It highlights the visual art invested in the brands and all the work that goes into producing the beer. This is achieved through purposeful typesetting, and layout of colour and graphics to achieve a laid–back, fun, but intuitive and enjoyable brand that works to support all the craft breweries and their fans in the Ottawa area.

Logo

The logo brings in the retro colours and fonts with a form and style and subtly resembles a beer label. The 613 creatively represents Ottawa, which also leaves for possibly of expansion by replacing the 613 with the city's own area code but still keep the branding.

Colours

The app's colours are a mixture of browns and yellows derived from a retro–style look and feel. The colours are warm, but delicious, emulating the array of colours and opaqueness found in craft beer.

Typography

Rockwell and Arvo are both slap serifs that are heavy, retro, mature but fun —perfectly capturing the essence of the app's brand. To contrast with it and ensure readability, Catamaran was chosen for its sharp edges and complimentary features.
